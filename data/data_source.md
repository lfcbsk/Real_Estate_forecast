# Data Source

**China Real Estate Demand Prediction**  
Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/china-real-estate-demand-prediction/data

> China's first real-estate demand prediction challenge — developing models to forecast new house transaction volume across property sectors.

## Notes

- Missing sector-month combinations indicate zero transactions (filled with 0).
- Zero rate across the full dataset is approximately 60%+.
- Data covers 96 sectors across multiple years with monthly frequency.
- To download: Kaggle account required. Use `kaggle competitions download -c china-real-estate-demand-prediction`.
